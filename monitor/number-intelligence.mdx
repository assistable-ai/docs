## Overview

Number Intelligence provides a high-level view of your phone numbers with monitoring and sorting capabilities. This feature helps you:

- Track phone number health
- Monitor call success rates
- Identify numbers that may need attention
- Sort and filter your number inventory

## Usage

Number Intelligence data is updated **once per month**. Navigate to the Number Intelligence section in your dashboard to view metrics and sort your numbers.

<Note>This feature is continuously being enhanced with additional metrics and more frequent updates.</Note>
