## Overview

For $97/month GHL users who have a single sub-account, you can connect directly to Assistable without the full agency workspace setup.

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up for Assistable">
    Create your Assistable account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Your Sub-Account">
    Follow the connection wizard to link your GHL sub-account directly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Permissions">
    Ensure all required permissions are enabled, including "Allow Custom Conversation Providers."
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>This connection method is specifically for GHL users on the $97 plan with direct sub-account access.</Note>
