## Overview

OAuth enables you to connect external GoHighLevel sub-accounts (those not natively under your GHL workspace) to your Assistable workspace. This is useful for agencies that manage clients across multiple GHL workspaces.

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to OAuth Settings">
    In your Assistable workspace, go to the OAuth configuration section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate OAuth Link">
    Create an OAuth authorization link for the external sub-account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize in GHL">
    Have the sub-account owner click the link and authorize the connection in GoHighLevel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Connection">
    Confirm the sub-account appears in your Assistable workspace dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>
