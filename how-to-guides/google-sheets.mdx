## Overview

Connect your AI assistant to Google Sheets to read data during conversations or write collected information to a spreadsheet. This is useful for dynamic pricing, inventory lookups, or data collection.

## Setup

Use a custom tool with a Google Sheets API endpoint or a middleware service like Zapier/Make to bridge the connection.
