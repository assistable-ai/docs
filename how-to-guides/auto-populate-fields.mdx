## Overview

Configure **Automatic Data Collection** in GoHighLevel to auto-fill custom fields using AI — without live tool calls. This captures data from voice calls (post-call) and chat interactions (per run), reducing manual input and eliminating tool call latency.

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to a Contact Record">
    Open any contact in GoHighLevel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Locate Automatic Data Collection">
    Scroll to the **Automatic Data Collection** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a New Rule">
    Click **Create New** and configure:
    - **Name**: Lowercase, no spaces (e.g., `business_ein`)
    - **Data Type**: `string` (most cases), `number`, or `boolean`
    - **Description**: What to capture (e.g., "User's Business EIN")
    - **Mapping**: Link to the appropriate custom field
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and Test">
    Send a test message (e.g., "Our CAC is $25 and LTV is $3,200") or complete a voice call. No tool call is triggered — latency stays normal. Refresh the contact to verify mapped fields are auto-filled.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>This method works alongside [Data Extraction](/how-to-guides/data-extraction) — use whichever approach fits your use case better.</Note>
