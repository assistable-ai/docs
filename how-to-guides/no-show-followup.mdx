## Overview

Create an automated workflow that detects appointment no-shows and triggers an AI follow-up call to reschedule. This uses GoHighLevel automation triggers combined with the Make AI Call action.

## Setup

1. Create a GHL workflow triggered by appointment status change to "no-show"
2. Add a delay (e.g., 15 minutes)
3. Add the **Make AI Call** action with your rescheduling assistant
4. Configure the assistant's prompt to acknowledge the missed appointment and offer to reschedule
