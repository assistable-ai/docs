## Overview

Assistable's automatic data extraction feature captures key information from conversations without requiring custom tools. The AI identifies and extracts data like names, emails, phone numbers, addresses, and custom fields during natural conversation.

## Setup

Configure extraction rules in your assistant's settings to define what data should be captured and where it should be stored in your CRM.
