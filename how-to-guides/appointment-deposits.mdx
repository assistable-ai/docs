## Overview

Automate deposit collection when clients book appointments. This reduces no-shows and streamlines the booking process using GoHighLevel's payment system and workflow automation.

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create the Deposit Product">
    1. Go to **Payments > Products** in GoHighLevel
    2. Click **+ Create Product**
    3. Name: "Appointment Deposit"
    4. Type: Digital Good / Service
    5. Price: Your desired amount (e.g., $50)
    6. Save
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate the Payment Link">
    1. Go to **Payments > Payment Links**
    2. Click **Create Payment Link**
    3. Select your deposit product
    4. Copy the generated URL
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the Booking Trigger Workflow">
    1. Go to **Automation > Workflows > Create New**
    2. Trigger: **Appointment Status → Confirmed**
    3. Action: **Send SMS**:
    
    *"Hi {{contact.first_name}}, your appointment is reserved! Secure your spot: [Payment Link]"*
    
    4. (Optional) Add a **Wait** step + reminder if unpaid after 24 hours
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the Payment Confirmation Workflow">
    1. Create a second workflow
    2. Trigger: **Order Submitted** (filter: Appointment Deposit product)
    3. Action: **Send SMS/Email**: "Thank you! Your deposit is received. Your appointment is fully secured."
    4. Action: **Update Contact Tag** → "Deposit Paid"
  </Step>
</Steps>
