## Overview

Your AI assistant can send SMS messages during a phone call — useful for sharing links, confirmations, or documents that are easier to send via text than dictate over the phone.

## Setup

Create a custom tool that triggers a GHL webhook to send an SMS. The AI will use this tool during calls when it needs to share a link or written information.
