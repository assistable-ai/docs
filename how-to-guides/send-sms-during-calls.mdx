## Overview

Enable your AI voice assistant to **send SMS messages during active phone calls** — perfect for sharing links, documents, or information that can't be conveyed over voice alone.

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create the Custom Tool">
    In your assistant settings:
    - **Name**: `sms_content`
    - **Description**: "Trigger when the user requests links, resources, or information via SMS during a call. Sends the requested information instantly."
    - Save (endpoint URL added in the next step)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Up the Automation Workflow">
    1. Go to **Automations → Create New Workflow**
    2. Name: "Custom Tool - Send SMS During Call"
    3. Add trigger: **Inbound Webhook**
    4. Copy the webhook URL and paste it into your custom tool's endpoint field
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure the SMS Action">
    In the workflow, add an **SMS** action after the webhook trigger. Configure the message body to include the link or information passed from the AI.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Your Prompt">
    Add instructions and the link to your AI prompt. Tell the assistant when to offer sending a text (e.g., when sharing URLs, booking links, or documents).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>This is the reverse of [Call from SMS](/build/custom-tools/call-from-sms) — there you go from text to voice, here you go from voice to text.</Tip>
