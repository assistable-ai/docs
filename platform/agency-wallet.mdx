## Overview

Your Agency Wallet is a prepaid balance that covers usage-based costs across all your sub-accounts — voice calls, messages, and other billable AI operations.

<Warning>**Critical**: If your agency wallet balance goes negative, **none of your sub-accounts** will be able to use inbound or outbound AI voice calling. Keep it funded!</Warning>

## Setting Up Your Wallet

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Billing Settings">
    Go to **Settings > Billing** in your workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Funds">
    Click **Add Balance** and enter the amount you want to fund. Funds are available immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable Auto-Pay">
    Turn on auto-pay to automatically refill your wallet when the balance drops below a threshold. This is **strongly recommended** to prevent service interruptions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How It Works

- Usage charges (voice minutes, messages) are deducted from your agency wallet in real time
- If [rebilling](/platform/rebilling) is enabled, your clients' wallets are charged at your set rates — and the base cost is deducted from your agency wallet
- You can monitor all transactions in the [Billing Portal](/platform/billing-portal)

<Tip>Set your auto-pay threshold higher than your typical daily usage to avoid any gaps in service.</Tip>
