## Overview

Subaccount limits let you control how much each sub-account can use — including the number of assistants, messages, phone numbers, and other resources. This is useful for managing plan tiers, preventing overuse, or restricting demo accounts.

## Configuring Limits

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the Sub-Account">
    In your workspace, find the sub-account you want to configure.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Manage Limits">
    Open the limits panel for that sub-account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Individual Limits">
    Configure maximum values for:
    - **Max Assistants** — How many AI assistants the account can create
    - **Max Messages** — Total message volume
    - **Phone Numbers** — Number of phone numbers allowed
    - Any other billable resources
    
    You can set specific numeric limits or mark each as **unlimited**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click save to apply. Limits take effect immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>Use limits in combination with [Snapshots](/platform/snapshots) to automatically set default limits for new sub-accounts based on their plan tier.</Tip>
