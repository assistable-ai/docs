## Overview

Each sub-account has its own wallet that funds their AI usage (voice calls, messages). Clients add funds to their wallet, and usage is billed at the rates you set via [Rebilling](/platform/rebilling).

## Prerequisites

Before a client can set up their wallet, they must be **invited as a member** to their sub-account. Without this, they won't see the billing settings.

<Note>
**Why can't my client see their billing settings?**

They need to be invited as a workspace member first. Go to your Agency Dashboard, find their sub-account, click **Invite Member**, and send an invite to their email. Once accepted, they'll see the Billing menu and can set up their wallet.
</Note>

## Client Wallet Setup

Once invited as a member, your client can:

1. Navigate to their sub-account's **Settings > Billing**
2. Add a payment card
3. Fund their wallet
4. Enable auto-pay for automatic refills

## How Usage Is Billed

- Usage charges are deducted from the sub-account's wallet at the rates you configured
- If the wallet runs dry, AI services pause until funds are added
- Transaction history is visible to the client in their billing portal

<Tip>If you want another user to access billing settings, they also need to be [added as a member](/platform/user-management) of the workspace.</Tip>
