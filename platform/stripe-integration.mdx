## Overview

Stripe integration is required for [Rebilling](/platform/rebilling). It allows your workspace to collect payments from clients through their wallet and process billing automatically.

## Connecting Stripe

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Rebilling Tab">
    Go to the **Rebilling** tab in your workspace navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Connect to Stripe">
    A Stripe OAuth modal opens in a new window.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Your Stripe Account">
    Choose the appropriate Stripe account and click **Connect**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the Integration">
    Stripe redirects you back to your workspace. You'll see the active Stripe connection displayed under the Rebilling tab.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Resetting the Connection

If you need to switch Stripe accounts or troubleshoot:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Break the Existing Connection">
    Go to the **Rebilling** tab (or **Integrations** tab) and click **Break Connection**.
    
    <Note>Breaking the connection does **not** affect previously saved billing data.</Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reconnect">
    Click **Connect to Stripe** again and follow the OAuth flow with your new account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## FAQs

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Will breaking the connection delete billing data?">
    No — it only resets the Stripe connection. All billing history remains intact.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I connect from either the Rebilling or Integrations tab?">
    Yes, both tabs provide the same connection process.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if the OAuth modal doesn't load?">
    Check your browser's pop-up blocker or try in a different browser.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
