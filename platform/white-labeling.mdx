## Overview

White labeling lets you rebrand the entire Assistable portal as your own product. Upload your logo, customize colors, set a custom domain, and even embed your own documentation — your clients never see the Assistable brand.

## What You Can Customize

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Logo** | Your company logo displayed across the portal |
| **Brand Colors** | Primary color applied globally to buttons, headers, and UI elements |
| **Custom Domain** | Your own domain (e.g., `ai.youragency.com`) instead of Assistable's |
| **Favicon** | Browser tab icon for your white-labeled portal |
| **Page Title** | Custom browser tab title |
| **iFrame Embed** | Embed landing pages or custom content |
| **Documentation URL** | Link to your own docs, shown as a "Docs" button in sub-accounts |

## Setup Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload Your Logo">
    Navigate to the **Branding** tab in your workspace. Click the logo upload area and upload your image (PNG, JPEG, or GIF — any size works, square recommended).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Brand Colors">
    Choose your primary brand color using the color picker. Click **Update Branding** to apply it globally across your workspace and all sub-accounts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Custom Domain">
    1. In the **Branding** tab, scroll to the **Domain** section
    2. Enter your custom domain (e.g., `ai.youragency.com`)
    3. In your DNS provider, add an **A Record** pointing to `72.72.71.71`
    4. Wait for DNS propagation and confirm the domain is connected
    5. Set your favicon and page title
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Documentation URL (Optional)">
    Paste your documentation page URL in the branding settings. Sub-accounts will see a "Docs" button linking to your docs.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## FAQs

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What file types work for logos?">
    PNG, JPEG, and GIF — any image format and size.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I embed a custom landing page?">
    Yes, use the iFrame embed option to display landing pages within the portal.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is the docs button visible to everyone?">
    Only if you provide a URL — then it appears in all sub-account views.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
