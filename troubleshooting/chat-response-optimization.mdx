## Overview

Optimize how quickly your AI responds to chat messages and how it handles message queuing.

## Tips

- **Reduce prompt complexity** — Shorter, focused prompts generate faster responses
- **Optimize knowledge base** — Remove redundant or overly large entries
- **Configure queue settings** — Adjust how the AI handles multiple incoming messages
- **Use Sleep Mode wisely** — Prevent conflicts between automated and AI messages
- **Monitor response times** — Track average response time in your dashboard
