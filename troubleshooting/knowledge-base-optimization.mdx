## Best Practices

- **Use FAQ format** for common questions — precise Q&A pairs improve accuracy significantly
- **Keep entries focused** — One topic per entry rather than long documents
- **Update regularly** — Outdated information causes incorrect responses
- **Test thoroughly** — Ask the AI questions that require knowledge base data and verify accuracy
- **Use specific language** — Avoid vague descriptions; be precise about services, pricing, and policies
- **Organize by category** — Group related entries for easier management
